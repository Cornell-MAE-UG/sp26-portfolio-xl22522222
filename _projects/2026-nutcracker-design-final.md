## Find
A design for a nutcracker that can crack a macademia nut

## Assume (from external sources):
- Average nut size is 1.5cm-3cm
- Average grip strength: Adult male--36-56kg, Adult female--21-35kg


## Given
- Average load to crack macadamia nut is 228.18kg

## Approach
- Find typical values for nuts, grip strength
- find dimensions of nut cracker to provide enough force while being usable with one hand
- determine the usage of a linear actuator

## Discussion
My nutcracker ended up being 26cm, which is slightly larger than the average nut cracker but was still fully usable and functional. It also met my requirements and is capable of supplying enough force to crack a macadamia nut of any reasonable size, between 1.5cm-3cm, which is the size of an average nut I got from the web. To achieve this, a grip strength of 40kg is required, which is fully possible for a very average adult male, while adult females and children may have to use both hands or use an additional tool. This design was the most optimal to maintain a simple design while maintaining a reasonable size and functionality.
